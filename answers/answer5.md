# ✅ Answer 5 – Subnetting /29 (32 subnets)

## Calculation
- Original: `192.168.1.0/24` → 256 addresses  
- Need 32 subnets → borrow 5 bits → **/29**  
- Subnet mask: **255.255.255.248**  
- Each subnet: **8 addresses → 6 usable**
